Grilled Cheese

Recipe Link

About:

This classic recipe has been a favorite of mine, and pretty much everyone for a very long time. Although I pulled it off the internet it can be assured most anyone you ask will know how to make a version of this delicious sandwich.

Directions:

  1. Combine butter and Parmesan cheese in bowl.
  2. Spread 1 side of each bread slice with 2 teaspoons butter mixture.
  3. Top 1 bread slice, buttered-side down, with 2 cheese slices. Place 1 bread slice, buttered-side up, over cheese.
  4. Heat 12-inch skillet or griddle over medium heat. Place 2 sandwiches into skillet; cook, turning once, 4-6 minutes or until golden brown and cheese is melted. Repeat with remaining sandwiches
